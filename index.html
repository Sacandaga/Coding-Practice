AboutMethodology & sources

Methodology

This simulation centers on a fictional 2025–2026 conflict scenario involving Houthi Red Sea attacks, U.S.–Iran hostilities, and principals-level decision-making. Documents are written from specific institutional perspectives (CJCS, CIA Director, independent analyst) using authentic formatting and analytical frameworks sourced from publicly available references including CJCSM 3160.01A, JP 3-60, NARA RG 243, and the Senate Intelligence Committee drone report.

All information derives from publicly available open sources. Fictional elements (HVT profiles, coordinates, source networks) are explicitly marked throughout.
